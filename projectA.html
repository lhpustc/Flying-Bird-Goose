User Instructions:
1. Click "SpeedUp" to accelerate the movement of the objects, and click "SpeedDown" to slow down their movements;
2. Click "RunStop" to stop/begin the dynamic change of the picture (including the movement of the objects and the variation of the colors);
3. Press "up-arrow" to move up the flying bird, and press "down-arrow" to move it down;
4. Press "right-arrow" to let the goose step forward, and press "left-arrow" to let it step backward;
5. Click at lower left near to the four-point star to make a five-point star appear, reclick at the same position to make it disappear;
6. Click at upper right near to it will result in the appearance of another five-point star;
7. Drag the mouse to rotate the four-point star;
8. Resize the browser window to change the opacity of the canvas.
